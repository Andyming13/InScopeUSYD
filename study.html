用于中英双语练习的词汇与情景任务 · Vocabulary & Scenarios for EN⇄ZH practice

常用词汇（第一次）· Core Vocabulary (Batch 1)

  • 你好 (nǐhǎo) – hello
  • 你好吗 (Nǐ hǎo ma) – how are you
  • 我的名字是 (Wǒ de míngzì shì) – My name is
  • 谢谢 (xièxie) – thank you
  • 多 (duō) – more
  • 少 (shǎo) – less
  • 地方 (dìfāng) – place
  • 吃 (chī) – eat
  • 喝 (hē) – drink
  • 好吃 (hǎochī) – tasty
  • 不好吃 (bù hào chī) – not delicious
  • 贵 (guì) – expensive
  • 便宜 (piányi) – cheap
  • 等位 (děng wèi) – wait
  • 人多 (rén duō) – crowded
Tip: 尝试把以上词汇套入下面的情景任务中进行双语表达。

情景任务 · Project Scenarios

1. Schedule Delay · 进度延迟

Project Situation：

Your food booth is not ready because the food ingredients were delivered too late. Your team needs to find a way to catch up with the schedule.

Vocabulary Bank：

时间 (time) · 快 (quick) · 慢 (slow) · deadline · schedule · milestone

Discussion Prompts：

  • EN→ZH: “We're running out of 时间!”
  • ZH→EN: “We are behind schedule.”
  • 共同：调整进度或设置新里程碑。

Goal：

Create 2–3 bilingual sentences that describe your problem and solution.

2. Budget Overrun · 预算超支

Project Situation：

You realize your drinks and decorations are too expensive. Your team’s budget is not enough to pay for everything.

Vocabulary Bank：

贵 (expensive) · 便宜 (cheap) · budget · cost control · resource allocation

Discussion Prompts：

  • EN→ZH: “It's too 贵!”
  • ZH→EN: “We have a budget overrun.”
  • 共同：减少装饰成本或购买更便宜的饮料。

Goal：

Create 2–3 bilingual sentences showing how you solve the budget issue.

3. Scope Change · 范围变更

Project Situation：

The organizers suddenly ask to add two new food booths. You don’t have enough space or time.

Vocabulary Bank：

多 (more) · 少 (less) · 地方 (place) · scope · change request · risk mitigation

Discussion Prompts：

  • EN→ZH: “Not enough 地方!”
  • ZH→EN: “This is a scope change.”
  • 共同：减少其他任务或重新规划布局。

Goal：

Create 2–3 bilingual sentences about your scope change and your team’s solution.

4. Quality Issue · 质量问题

Project Situation：

Some of your food ingredients are spoiled or taste bad. Your team must find a way to maintain food quality and safety.

Vocabulary Bank：

好吃 (tasty) · 不好吃 (not delicious) · quality · inspection · mitigation plan

Discussion Prompts：

  • EN→ZH: “It's 不好吃!”
  • ZH→EN: “We have a food quality issue.”
  • 共同：开业前进行食材质检。

Goal：

Create 2–3 bilingual sentences to explain your food quality issue and solution.

5. Stakeholder Communication · 利益相关者沟通

Project Situation：

During the Food Festival, some customers are complaining about long waiting times and expensive food. Your team needs to manage communication, collect feedback, and improve service quality.

Vocabulary Bank：

人太多了 (crowded) · 等待 (wait) · stakeholder engagement · feedback · improvement

Discussion Prompts：

  • EN→ZH: “The customer 等待 too long!”
  • ZH→EN: “We received customer complaints.”
  • 共同：优化服务流程并收集反馈。

Goal：

Create 2–3 bilingual sentences describing the complaint and your solution.

6. Schedule Delay · 进度延迟（再练习）

Goal：Create 2–3 bilingual sentences that describe your problem and solution.

同第 2 题场景，重复用于加深练习。

Goal：Create 2–3 bilingual sentences showing how you solve the budget issue.
