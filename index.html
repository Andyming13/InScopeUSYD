How It Works

Simple, structured, and social. Sessions run weekly during term.

Step 1
Pick a language table

Choose your target language and level. Hosts guide small-group conversation.

Step 2
Share & practice

Short prompts, role-plays, and games keep it fun and inclusive.

Step 3
Reflect & connect

Exchange tips, get resources, and make friends from around the world.

About the Project

InScopeI1 is a not-for-profit, student-run initiative at the University of Sydney that celebrates International Mother Language Day values year-round.

We believe language is a bridge between cultures. By embracing the diversity of languages, we open up new avenues for understanding and collaboration.
